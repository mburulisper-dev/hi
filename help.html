Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

+

Click on "Get Started" or "Register" button. Fill in your details including full name, email, phone number, and password. Select whether you're a student or landlord, and you're all set!

How do I search for apartments?

+

Log in to your account and go to "Browse Apartments". Use the search bar to filter by location, price range, and room type. You can also filter by amenities like WiFi, Water, Parking, and more.

How do I contact a landlord?

+

Click on an apartment to view details. You'll see a "Message Landlord" button. Click it to send a message directly to the landlord. You can ask questions about the apartment, availability, or anything else.

How do I save an apartment?

+

On each apartment card, you'll see a heart icon. Click it to save the apartment to your saved list. You can view all saved apartments in your profile by clicking "Saved Apartments".

How do I leave a review?

+

Go to the apartment details page. Scroll down to "Add Review" section. Enter your name, write your review (minimum 10 characters), and select a rating from 1-5 stars. Click "Submit Review" and your review will be visible to other students.

How do landlords list apartments?

+

Landlords can log in and go to their dashboard. Click "Add Apartment" and fill in the details including title, location, price, room type, amenities, and upload an image. Once added, the apartment will be visible to students.

Is my data safe on Nyumba Find?

+

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal data. We never share your information with third parties without your consent. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

What should I do if I find a suspicious listing?

+

If you see a listing that looks suspicious or violates our guidelines, please contact us immediately at support@nyumbafind.com with the apartment details and reasons for reporting.

How do I reset my password?

+

On the login page, click "Forgot Password?" Enter your email address. You'll receive an email with a link to reset your password. Click the link and create a new password.

Can I delete my account?

+

Yes, you can request account deletion from your account settings. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

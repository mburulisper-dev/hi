About Nyumba Find

Making housing accessible and transparent for students across Kenya

Our Story

Nyumba Find was founded in 2024 with a simple mission: to make finding student accommodation in Kenya easier, faster, and more transparent. We realized that many students were struggling to find safe, affordable housing and that landlords had limited platforms to reach them.

Today, we're proud to be one of the leading apartment finding platforms in Kenya, connecting thousands of students with their perfect homes every month.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, transparent, and user-friendly platform that connects students with verified landlords and quality housing options across Kenya.
